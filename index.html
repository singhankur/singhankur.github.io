My Belief

Responsible AI

AI is the most powerful tool our generation has built — and with that comes real responsibility. I believe that every AI system we ship must be explainable, auditable, and human-centred. Whether it's an LLM automating security triage or an agentic system making recommendations, there must always be a human in the loop for high-stakes decisions.

In my work, I apply this by designing AI systems with clear override mechanisms, logging every model decision for auditability, and ensuring teams understand why a model behaves the way it does — not just that it works. Good AI engineering is not just about accuracy metrics; it's about trust, fairness, and the impact on people.

I am committed to building AI that amplifies human capability without replacing human judgement — and to mentoring the next generation of engineers to hold the same standard.
